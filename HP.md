# HOME PAGE STRUCTURE PROMPT

Create a premium modern fitness platform homepage.

The homepage should feel energetic, professional, trustworthy, and recruiter-attractive.

Avoid generic SaaS layouts.

Use storytelling and visual hierarchy.

Follow my color system strictly.

---

## SECTION 1 — HERO SECTION

Design a unique split-screen hero section.

Left Side:

* Premium headline
* Short description
* Explore Classes button
* Become Trainer button

Right Side:

* Large fitness illustration/image
* Floating stats cards
* Active Members
* Expert Trainers
* Classes Completed
* Success Rate

Requirements:

* Green gradient background
* Glassmorphism stat cards
* Motion effects
* Premium SaaS feel
